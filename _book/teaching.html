2 Teaching

During term time I teach on a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate courses including:

  • First year team projects: COMP101 led by Ulrike Sattler
  • Mentoring one group of six first year students

2.2 Second year

  • Second year software engineering: COMP23311 led by Suzanne Embury especially our software engineering mentoring program
  • Leading second year tutorials and being a second year tutor to a group of six students

2.3 Penultimate year

  • Course leader for the industrial experience elective course (year in industry) and COMP2CARS (second year tutorials)

2.4 Final year

  • Supervising a handful of final year educational projects based in secondary schools in Manchester, see git.io/computinged.

2.5 Masters

  • Supervising Master of Science projects in Data Science, particularly subjects relating to Wikipedia, Wikdata and the use of chatbots.
