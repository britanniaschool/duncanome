B.1 The Class of 1948, 1970, 2003 and 2048

A journey through the time, stopping at the Computer Science classes of 2003, 1970, 1948 and 2048 in Manchester, with pictures and commentary.

Computers have been with us for a while, but let’s start in 2003: Figure B.1 shows students of the MSc Advanced Computer Science - Foundation route class of 2003. The people in the picture are smiling because they are being entertained by Richard Giordano, who took this picture while teaching.

Figure B.1: The class of 2003. Most of these people have moved on, although some stayed on to do research including Matthew Horridge, Michael Parkin, Karim Nashar and myself (the one in the back row with the dodgy haircut)
